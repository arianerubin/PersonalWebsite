MY PATH

My Professional and Personal Journey

Exploring Healthcare Web Development

Welcome to the intersection of healthcare and technology! I'm Ariane Aoki Rubin, a passionate Healthcare web developer with a background in Dietetics. Equipped with dual master's degrees in Sports Medicine and Maternal and Children Nutrition from Universidade de Sao Paulo (USP), I've further refined my expertise by specializing in Pediatric Food Allergy through FARE North America.

I am a Brazilian immigrant, now residing in the USA, much like many of you. I'm accompanied by my three beautiful daughters - 13-year-old Giovana, 3-year-old Olivia, and 8-month-old Sophia, along with my husband, two dogs and a cat. They've been my constant companions, witnessing my transition into healthcare web development.

I'm fueled by the opportunity to bridge the gap between healthcare and cutting-edge technology. My goal is to enhance patient care, streamline processes, and empower healthcare professionals through innovative web development and software solutions. Join me as we navigate the constantly evolving landscape of healthcare technology, shaping a brighter and healthier future for all.
